ART,

As of mid 2021 I began experimenting with acrylic paintings. I found working on canvas rolls the most enjoyable as I could make large scale paintings and work on the floor allowing me to get up extra close to the art. It also allowed for free flowing movements.

To date I have only completed one major art piece of the female form and developing the next. Working with the female form is always inspiring to me and I love the beauty and rawness that comes with the human form as a whole.

The power these pieces hold in the simplicity of the female form. The feminine appearing strong yet calm, powerful yet in flow. I believe this to be true of the feminine and the power the feminine holds in all forms.
